Romanian Mamaliga Recipe

Description

Delicious Romanian Recipe, easy to do and ready in 20 minutes!

Ingredients

  • 250g malai(polenta)
  • 1 liter water
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • Optional: 1 spoon of butter
  • Optional: Sour cream - for serving
  • Optional: cottage cheese - for serving

Steps

  1. Put the water to boil and add the salt
  2. When the water boils, slowly put the 250g malai(polenta) and don't stop stirring with a whisk
  3. Let it cook for 15-20 minutes(or more if it needs), and stirr with a wooden spoon every ~5 minutes or more often
  4. Do the taste check, and if it's soft it means it's done! Add the butter and stirr for 1-2 minutes until you can no longer see it/feel it while stirring
  5. Turn off the stove and prepare the plates to serve. All you need additionally is sour cream and cottage cheese. Enjoy!
