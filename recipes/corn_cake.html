Corn cake Recipe

Description

Delicious Romanian Recipe of Corn Cake

Ingredients

  • 1 cup white flour
  • 2 cups polenta
  • 1 cup of sugar
  • 1/2 cup of oil
  • 1 teaspoon of vanilla essence
  • 1 baking powder
  • 2 eggs
  • 400-500g of Joghurt

Steps

  1. Mix all the dry ingredients in a bowl, and then add the rest of them and stirr very well
  2. Put the composition in a tray that has oven paper on it and place it in the oven for 35-40 minutes at around 180g Celcius
  3. Try to see if it's cooked with a wooden stick, and if there's nothing on it after putting it in the cake, it means that it's ready
  4. Enjoy!
